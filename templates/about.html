About PAL

PAL is a Personal Assistant here to help you! PAL's modular back-end makes PAL readily extensible. Right now PAL can handle questions / commands related to Bon Appetit dining service, word definitions or synonyms, pizza, Facebook posts, movies, translations, weather, math, and nearby shops or locations.

For a comprehensive list of PAL's services, see our readme.

Things to Try

  • find nearby bars
  • what's the weather like tomorrow?
  • order a medium pizza with olives
  • what are the laws of robotics?
  • how far away is the moon?
